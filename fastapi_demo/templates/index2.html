Note: If you do not get back a video result, it most likely means that the face detector could not detect faces in all the input video frames. This can sometimes happen for animated movie clips and videos recorded using the “Record Video” feature. The working of the “Record Video” feature highly depends on the camera quality and the resolution. Keep the video and audio short to minimize compute latency. It may take some time (not more than a minute usually) to generate the results. This interactive site is only a user-friendly demonstration of the bare minimum capabilities of the Emotional Talking faces model.
